OFFICIAL GUIDE

THE BASICS

MOVEMENT: Use the WASD keys to navigate the map.

FATAL CONTACT: Any collision with enemies results in immediate DEATH.

THE DASH

ATTACK: DASH using the Left Mouse Button (LMB).

RECHARGE RATE: You need to wait 1 second to dash again.

COMBOS

SLAUGHTER EFFICIENCY: Eliminating multiple targets within a single Dash increases your SCORE.

CHARGE GENERATION: After eliminating 5 or more hostiles in one strike generates one Frenzy Charge. They are shown in upper HUD.

FRENZY MODE

ACTIVATION: After generating 3 Charges triggers the FRENZY state automatically.

ENHANCEMENTS: During Frenzy, player movement speed is significantly increased and enemy movement speed decreased. The Dash recharge rate is reduced to 0.6 seconds for the duration of the state.

DIFFICULTY

ADAPTIVE DIFFICULTY: The arena security level increases based on your kill count. Enemies will be getting increased movement speed at 50 and 100 points.
